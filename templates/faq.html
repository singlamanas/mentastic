What are the different modes of payment that I can use?

We offer all sorts of payment modes : Cash on Delivery, UPI and Mobile Wallets, Credit Card, Debit Card and Netbanking
What is the expected time of shipment?
Generally, your items will be shipped to your delivery address within 2-3 working days of completing the order on our website. You will be notified as well about your delivery status.
Can I return my product if I do not like it?
You can always return the product if you do not like it and a full refund would be processed if returned before 30 days of purchase and an exchange would be made after 30 days. No return would be allowed after 60 days.
Is my personal data secure on the Mentastic website?
We are 100% committed to the security of our customers. No personal information and details are leaked by Mentastic to any other company. However we do analyse the user activity for better recommendation of products.
